Overview

Our family apartment is in the beautiful Dalmatian village of Vinišće. The apartment is fully furnished and equipped with everything you need for a peaceful vacation. It is on the ground floor with a charming south-facing half-covered terrace and a lovely pool just off the terrace. The apartment has a private parking space on site.

The apartment complex contains four units. Each unit has three apartments. In total, twelve apartments can enjoy the shared pool area. By the pool area, there are sunbeds and lounge sets that everyone in the complex can use. The Vinišće pebble beach is only 200 m from the apartment. The water is crystal clear and offers sailing and windsurfing opportunities. You can rent a boat, visit small islands and enjoy the wild beaches.

Spend your day at the nearby beach, relax by the pool or have a drink on the terrace.

Nearby the apartment is six restaurants, several small bakeries, homemade ice cream shops, and night cafes. Daily you have a market with fresh fruits, vegetables, and fish caught just hours before. Vinišće is ideal for families with children and people who want to enjoy the beautiful nature and crystal clear Adriatic sea. Everything you need is just around the corner. The apartment offers the privacy and silence you need, but it is close to every place you want to enjoy in the village.

The apartment is non-smoking inside. Some of our guests may also have pet allergies. Therefore, we are sorry that you cannot bring your pets.
